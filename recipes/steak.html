Steak

Description

Here’s how to cook steak like a chef – pan seared and basted with garlic thyme butter! It’s dramatic, simple
cooking at its best, you’ll look like a total pro and feel like you’re dining at the best steakhouse in town

Ingredients

  • 1 - 2 boneless ribeye or scotch fillet
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 75g / 5 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 6 sprigs fresh thyme or 3 sprigs rosemary
  • 5 garlic cloves
